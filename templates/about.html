The homeowner's buddies!

You know that receipt on the door you bought last year that you'd really need right now for warranties? Yeah it's probably in a drawer somewhere, where you will never find it, does it sound familiar?

House buddy lets you store receipts, documents, and other information so that you'll always know where you have them.

Custom categories

We know that not everybody's lives and homes are the same, and that is why we let you create your own categories, making them custom to you and your needs.

The Blog!

Kiela runs the House buddy blog, where you can find usefull tips on anything house related, she also blogs about her everyday life renovating houses.

Want to become a house buddy blogger and share your amazing ideas and hacks aswell? Get in touch today!

Benefits:

Easy and quick receipt storing

You can easily add an image and information about a receipt from your phone.

You can add custom categories

You can customize your page to fit your needs, it doesn't have to be a receipt storing site, it can be whatever you'd like, maybe you want to start a cat picture organizer, who are we to stop you?

Have access to your information wherever you are

If you save all of your color codes in House Buddy, you can access them while at the hardware store, and never have to second guess what the name of the color in the kitchen was again.

Save time and money

Having it all in one easily accessed place saves you time, and House buddy is completely free to use, so why pay for expensive cloud services?
